Our Story

Crafting authentic Neapolitan pizzas since 1994, honoring centuries-old Italian baking techniques.

Dough, Tomatoes, and Love

At Pizza Classic, our dough undergoes a slow 48-hour cold fermentation process, resulting in a light, airy, and perfectly charred crust. We source our flour directly from mills in Campania, Italy.

Our pizza sauce contains only 100% organic San Marzano tomatoes, crushed by hand with a pinch of sea salt. No preservatives, no added sugars. Just the raw, sweet taste of nature.
